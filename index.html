🌱 Foster or Kill Innovation?

A fast, phone‑friendly culture game

How to Play

  • You will see 5 short scenarios about workplace behaviour.
  • Choose: ✅ Foster Innovation or 🚫 Kill Innovation.
  • Each correct answer = 100 points (maximum 500).
  • After each answer, you will see a short explanation to help you learn.
  • Questions get harder as you play: easy → easy → medium → medium → hard.

💡 Tip: Take your time. Learning is more important than speed!
